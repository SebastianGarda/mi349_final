Bio

My name is Sebastian Garda, and this is my portfolio/website! I am an aspiring game developer who is currently in my junior year of college at Michigan State University, majoring in Media and Information with a minor in Game Design and Development. This website, as stated, was made as a portfolio to show off my games that I've created for classes here at MSU, as well as to serve as my final project for MI349 Web Design and Development.

My passion in game design is level design specifically. I enjoy the intricacies of teaching a player as they play. Figuring out how to do that is like a puzzle to me. I also love character design as I find, for me at least, it's one of the primary things that initially draws me to a game and keeps me interested and invested. I am practiced in pixel art as well as physical pencil and paper art and am teaching myself how to use blender. I am also looking to get better at digital art using Clip Studio Paint. I have extensive experience with the Unity game engine thanks to my 3 years of education at MSU.

My family moved around quite a bit when I was a kid, so i'm very much used to adapting to new environments and being the new face around. I find it very easy to meet new people and get integrated. I find myself to be a social chameleon, and I appreciate talking and listening to people and getting to know them better.

My hobbies are playing video games, listening to music, watching movies and tv shows, drawing, reading, and collecting figures from games I enjoy. I hope one day to be able to own a physical copy of a game I worked on and helped publish, that I can show to my freinds and family.
